About

I'm a mathematician studying algebraic geometry, especially derived categories of algebraic varieties and positive characteristic geometry. I am currently an NSF Postdoct at UC Berkeley supervised by Martin Olsson. From September 2022 until September 2024 I was a postdoc of Lenny Taelman at the University of Amsterdam, although in Fall 2023 I was working at the Hausdorff Research Institute for Mathematics in Bonn as part of the Junior Trimester Program Algebraic geometry: derived categories, Hodge theory, and Chow groups. Before that I was a PhD student of Johan de Jong at Columbia University.
